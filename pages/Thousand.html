Beyond 1000

Ten Thousands
is named Ten Thousand and is written as 10000
To write this on a stone would use writing that would be too small to see and it would be even worse for 100000 and so on.

For numbers beyond 1000 the following the following symbols are used

Ten the number of 0s in Ten 10 is 1

Hundred the number of 0s in Hundred 100 is 2

Thousand the number of 0s in Thousand 1000 is 3

Ten Thousand the number of 0s in Ten Thousand 10000 is 4

Hundred Thousand the number of 0s in Hundred Thousand 100000 is 5

Million the number of 0s in Million 1000000 is 6
