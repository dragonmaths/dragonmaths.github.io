Taking Away

Taking away, or subtracting to be mathematical, is done by blue dragons.

–1 represents a blue dragon taking away one red stone

–(-1) or –-1 represent a blue dragon taking away one blue stone

–-1 should never be used. ‘Take away minus one’ should always be written as –(-1)
This stops the take away and minus sign merging.
