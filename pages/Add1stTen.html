Add to Twenty

Since counting is done in tens it is important to know how ten is made up.

1+9 = 10            10–1 = 9

2+8 = 10           10–2 = 8

3+7 = 10            10–3 = 7

4+6 = 10            10–4 = 6

5+5 = 10           10–5 = 5

6+4 = 10            10–6 = 4

7+3 = 10            10–7 = 3

8+2 = 10           10–8 = 2

9+1 = 10            10–9 = 1


Learn these and arithmetic becomes much easier.

Knowing how to add all the numbers from 1 to 9 to each other also helps a lot.
